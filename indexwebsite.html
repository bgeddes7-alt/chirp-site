How it works
Simple for parents.
Fun for kids.

Set it up in 5 minutes. Kids are motivated to help around the house. Parents stop being the bad guys.

1

Parents set tasks

Create a list of tasks — chores, homework, reading, anything. Assign minutes as rewards. Takes 2 minutes to set up.

2

Kids complete & submit

Kids see their tasks on their own screen, mark them done, and tap submit. They can track their balance in real time.

3

Time unlocks automatically

Parent approves with one tap. Screen time unlocks on their device — or their Nintendo Switch. No timers to set.

Real enforcement
Not the honor system.

Chirp integrates directly with your devices. When time runs out, apps lock. When kids earn more, they unlock. Automatically.
