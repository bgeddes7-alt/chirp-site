1. Who we are

Chirp is a screen time management application operated by Geddes Ventures LLC, a California limited liability company ("we," "us," or "our"). Our contact email is hello@chirp-kids.com.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Chirp web application and any related services (collectively, the "Service").

2. Children's privacy (COPPA)

✅ Our COPPA-compliant design

Chirp is designed so that only parents or legal guardians (age 18+) create accounts and provide personal information. Children use Chirp as sub-profiles within their parent's account. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Children do not register, log in, or submit personal data — they access Chirp only through a parent-controlled interface.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, please contact us immediately at hello@chirp-kids.com and we will delete that information promptly.

Child sub-profiles contain only a first name (or nickname) assigned by the parent, and a task/minutes balance associated with the parent's account. This information is entered by the parent, not the child.

3. Information we collect

Information you provide to us

  • Account information: Your email address and password when you create a parent account.
  • Child profile information: First names or nicknames you assign to child sub-profiles under your account.
  • Task and activity data: Tasks you create, approval decisions you make, screen time minutes earned and spent.
  • Nintendo Switch connection (optional): If you choose to connect your Nintendo account, we store a session token and access token on your behalf to communicate with Nintendo's parental controls API. We store the device ID and device name associated with your Switch. We never store your Nintendo account password.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, timestamps of actions. Used to improve the Service.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and device type for compatibility purposes.
  • Log data: IP address, browser type, and pages accessed, collected automatically by our hosting provider (Vercel) and database provider (Supabase).

Information we do NOT collect

  • Payment information (we have no paid tier in operation during beta)
  • Location data beyond what's inferred from IP address
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Any personal information entered by children

4. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and child sub-profiles
  • Operate the task approval and screen time balance features
  • Communicate with Nintendo's parental controls API on your behalf (if connected)
  • Send push notifications for task submissions and approvals
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmations, password resets)
  • Improve the Service based on usage patterns
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We will not use your information for advertising, and we will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Nintendo Switch integration

Chirp's Nintendo Switch integration is entirely optional. If you choose to connect your Nintendo account:

  • You authorize Chirp to modify your Nintendo Switch parental control settings (specifically, the daily play time limit) on your behalf.
  • We store your Nintendo session token and access token in our secure database (Supabase) to perform these modifications without requiring you to re-authenticate each time.
  • We modify parental control settings only when a child in your account starts or ends a screen time session.
  • You can disconnect your Nintendo account at any time within the Chirp settings, which will delete your stored tokens from our database.
Note: Chirp's Nintendo integration uses Nintendo's official parental controls API. Chirp is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Nintendo Co., Ltd. Nintendo Switch is a trademark of Nintendo.

6. Data sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use Supabase (database and authentication) and Vercel (hosting). These providers process data on our behalf and are contractually required to protect it.
  • Nintendo: When you use the Nintendo integration, your Nintendo session token is used to make API calls to Nintendo's servers.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information is subject to a materially different privacy policy.

7. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion of your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or compliance purposes.

Anonymized or aggregated data (e.g., aggregate usage statistics) may be retained indefinitely.

8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure database access controls through Supabase, and row-level security policies that prevent one user from accessing another's data.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your rights and choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out of communications: Unsubscribe from non-essential emails at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@chirp-kids.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. California residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at hello@chirp-kids.com.
